what is the collective?

Taylor Shots Collective is a community of photographers and videographers designed to help one another find gigs and collaborate with other creators. The purpose of this collective is to help talented and undiscovered creators find clients and develop skills through a community who shares the same goal. As this community grows, we strive to still make undiscovered creators the top priority so they can find other experienced creators and new clients to help them grow their network and ultimately live out their dreams.
